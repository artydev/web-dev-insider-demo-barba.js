What is it?

It is a demo of a traditional, standard website. Content does not matter. Just click through the links to sub pages on this website. You will notice that nothing is being loaded dynamically (sub pages), no transitions.
It is just a website like million others.

Alternatively, for better result and the UX, you could embed Barba.js library to website like this one to achieve absolutely smooth effect, without reloading a browser.
You could build a unique website like a real Maybach. Having spent 30 min!

You could do that using Angular, Vue.js or React of course. At least a few hours needed... I would like to show you that you do not have to load huge framework to create that result (and spend long time on it!). That's the purpose of the whitepaper that you can download here 100% for free.

You should compare this website with the dynamic one here. Can you feel the difference?
